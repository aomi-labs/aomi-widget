# iOS App

<Callout type="info">
**Coming Soon** — The Aomi iOS app is currently in development. Follow us on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/aomilabs) to get notified when it launches.
</Callout>

## What to Expect

The iOS app will bring the full Aomi experience to your iPhone:

- **Native experience** — A fast, fluid interface designed for iOS.
- **Push notifications** — Get alerts for transactions, price movements, and more.
- **Face ID / Touch ID** — Secure wallet access with biometrics.
- **Wallet integration** — Connect existing wallets or use the built-in wallet.
- **Offline support** — Review your portfolio and history without an internet connection.

## Features

- **Voice input** — Speak your commands instead of typing.
- **Widgets** — Check balances and prices from your home screen.
- **Shortcuts** — Integrate with Siri Shortcuts for quick actions.
- **Apple Watch** — (Planned) Quick glances and approvals from your wrist.
