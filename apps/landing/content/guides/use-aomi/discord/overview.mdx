# Discord Bot

<Callout type="info">
**Coming Soon** — The Aomi Discord bot is currently in development. Join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/aomi) to get notified when it launches.
</Callout>

## What to Expect

The Discord bot will bring the same AI capabilities available on Web App and Telegram to your Discord server:

- **Natural language commands** — Ask questions, execute trades, and manage your portfolio through chat.
- **Server integration** — Add the bot to your community server for shared access.
- **Slash commands** — Quick access to wallet, model selection, and App switching.
- **Channel-aware** — Configure which channels the bot responds in.
- **Wallet connectivity** — Connect your wallet and sign transactions directly from Discord.

## Use Cases

- **Community support** — Let community members ask questions and get instant AI-powered answers.
- **Trading channels** — Execute swaps and check prices without leaving Discord.
- **Alpha groups** — Research tokens and analyze market data collaboratively.
